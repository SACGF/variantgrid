{% page_help_embedded title="My Lab Stats" %} This page contains graphs that show the top 10 genes with the most VUS for both total classifications and unique alleles. By default the graphs display the top 10 genes, however, all genes with the same number of VUS as the 10th position gene will also be displayed. Only germline classifications are currently displayed in these graphs.

The buttons below the total classifications graph allow you display classifications of all clinical significances for a gene and display VUS sub-classifications (VUS A, VUS B, VUS C).

To exclude specific clinical significance values from the plot, you can click on that value in the legend.
