Survey Invitation: PanelApp Australia and Shariant Platforms

Genomics Australia invites members of the genomics sector to participate in a survey regarding the utility and sustainability of the PanelApp Australia and Shariant platforms. The survey is open to both users and non-users of the platforms.

Your feedback will inform future planning for these resources. Many questions are optional, and you are encouraged to share the survey link with colleagues. All responses will be treated confidentially; aggregated findings may be shared with relevant stakeholders. A summary of results will be provided to participants.

The survey will be available for three weeks from 3 November to 23 November 2025.
