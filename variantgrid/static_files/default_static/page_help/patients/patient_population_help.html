We use gnomAD labels with additional Australian specific groups: 'Australo Melanesian', 'Polynesian', 'South East Asian', 'Middle East / North African'

A person may belong to multiple groups, for instance President Obama would have an entry for both African/African American and Non-Finnish European.

The typical white person is Non-Finnish European. Australo Melanesian = Papuans, Aboriginal Australians and Melanesians.
